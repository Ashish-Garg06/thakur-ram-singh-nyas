About the Trust

Founded in Remembrance,
Built for Service

Thakur Ram Singh Smriti Nyas, Himachal Pradesh is a public charitable institution established in the revered memory of Shraddheya Thakur Ram Singh Ji — a राष्ट्रऋषि who dedicated his life to nation, society, and cultural awakening.

Every initiative of the Trust draws strength from his ideals of seva, discipline, integrity, and unwavering commitment to social welfare.

सेवा परमो धर्मः — Service is the Highest Dharma
