About the Trust

Founded in Remembrance,
Built for Service

Thakur Ram Singh Smriti Nyas, Himachal Pradesh is a public charitable institution established with a vision to serve society through meaningful, sustainable, and value-driven initiatives — founded in the revered memory of Shraddheya Thakur Ram Singh Ji.

His life exemplified selfless service, integrity, discipline, and an unwavering commitment to the welfare of society. His ideals form the moral and philosophical foundation upon which the Trust carries forward its work.

"सर्वे भवन्तु सुखिनः, सर्वे सन्तु निरामयाः।
सर्वे भद्राणि पश्यन्तु, मा कश्चिद् दुःखभाग्भवेत्॥"

Rooted in the ethos of Bharatiya Sanskriti, the Trust operates on the principles that true development is the holistic upliftment of individuals and communities — socially, culturally, and spiritually.

Our Vision

Guiding Principles

सेवा परमो धर्मः — Service is the Highest Dharma
01
Education for All
To promote education by establishing and supporting schools, colleges, coaching centers, and providing scholarships to deserving and needy students without discrimination.
02
Universal Healthcare
To establish hospitals, dispensaries, and organize medical camps, while promoting health awareness, yoga, and physical fitness for the general well-being of all.
03
Sustainable Development
To undertake rural development activities including agriculture, self-employment, livelihood generation, and environmental protection through conservation and awareness.
04
Inclusive Society
To provide assistance to poor, needy, and underprivileged sections including women, children, elderly, and differently-abled, while organizing cultural programs promoting social harmony.
05
Nation Building
To promote research, knowledge dissemination, and charitable activities for the benefit of society — without discrimination of caste, creed, or religion — contributing to Rashtra Nirman.

Our Founder

The Inspiration Behind
Every Initiative

The Trust draws continuous inspiration from the life of Shraddheya Thakur Ram Singh Ji, a figure who devoted his existence to the service of society. His principles of seva (service), integrity, and social responsibility form the moral and philosophical backbone of the Trust's work.

His ideals remind us that true leadership is not about power or position, but about the depth of one's commitment to the people. Every initiative undertaken by the Trust is a step towards honoring that legacy.

"Let a person uplift oneself through one's own efforts — and through that elevation, serve the world."

The Trust combines his traditional wisdom with contemporary methods, ensuring development remains rooted in cultural values while addressing modern challenges. Community participation and collective responsibility are at the heart of this approach.

What We Do

Activities & Focus Areas

The Trust combines its core value system with real on-ground service, creating a balanced identity of heritage and action.

01
📚
Education & Skill Development
Promoting quality education and skill development among youth — enhancing learning opportunities, academic support, and building capacities that empower individuals to become self-reliant.
विद्या ददाति विनयं, विनयाद् याति पात्रताम्।
पात्रत्वात् धनमाप्नोति, धनात् धर्मं ततः सुखम्॥
02
🏥
Healthcare & Well-being
Improving community health through awareness programs, preventive care initiatives, and promotion of physical and mental well-being for a healthier society.
आरोग्यं परमं भाग्यं, स्वास्थ्यं सर्वार्थसाधनम्।
03
🌾
Rural Development & Livelihood
Strengthening livelihoods, promoting self-sufficiency, and improving socio-economic conditions of rural communities across Himachal Pradesh.
कृषिर्वलम् — Agriculture is the strength of the nation.
04
🌿
Environmental Conservation
Promoting environmental awareness, plantation drives, water conservation, and responsible use of natural resources — emphasizing harmony with nature.
माता भूमिः पुत्रोऽहं पृथिव्याः
05
🐄
Animal Welfare & Gaushala
Supporting protection and care of animals, especially indigenous cattle, recognizing their cultural, ecological, and economic significance in Indian society.
गावो विश्वस्य मातरः — Cows are the mothers of the world.
06
🤝
Social Welfare & Community
Working for the upliftment of underprivileged and marginalized sections of society — women, children, elderly, and differently-abled — promoting equality and dignity.
परहितार्थं इदं शरीरम्
07
🇮🇳
Youth Empowerment
Special emphasis on empowering youth through physical, intellectual, and moral development — recognizing them as the foundation of a strong and self-reliant nation.
उत्तिष्ठत जाग्रत प्राप्य वरान्निबोधत
08
🎭
Culture & Relief
Organizing social and cultural community programs promoting harmony, cultural values, and undertaking relief activities during natural calamities and emergencies.
निःस्वार्थ सेवा — Selfless Service Always
